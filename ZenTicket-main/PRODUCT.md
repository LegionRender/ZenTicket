# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Operators, administrators, and business owners use ZenTicket to capture receipts, monitor fiscal automation, review invoices, and manage account or connector settings during task-focused work sessions.

## Product Purpose

ZenTicket helps users convert ticket data into fiscal workflows, track operational status, and manage AI-assisted billing limits and connectors. Success means users can scan, review, correct, and administer billing operations without second-guessing interface states.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel operational and polished, with clear hierarchy and restrained use of the ZenTicket blue accent.

## Anti-references

Avoid over-decorated product controls, mismatched button styles, broad CSS overrides that change unrelated controls, and heavy full-saturation accents on inactive or secondary actions.

## Design Principles

- Use one clear component vocabulary for repeated actions.
- Reserve strong blue treatment for primary actions and selected states.
- Keep secondary actions visible but quiet.
- Preserve dense operational scanning without making every control compete.
- Prefer explicit reusable classes over broad style overrides.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target practical WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard focus states, reduced-motion compatibility, and color-independent affordances through borders, labels, and icons.
